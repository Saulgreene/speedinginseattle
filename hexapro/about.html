Legal Team

The legal team at Speeding in Seattle, is at your service. Life is busy and it's not always convenient to drop everything for a meeting, to request a hearing, or show up in court. We can get your case expertly handled and easily completed online, through email, or by phone. We offer reliable advice and representation in legal matters concerning using a vehicle in your daily life, from traffic tickets to reckless driving to personal injury. Mitch Greene has 25 years of experience and in-depth knowledge about Washington traffic law. The goal of our office is to keep your driving record clean. We strive to make this a quick, efficient, and hassle-free process.
